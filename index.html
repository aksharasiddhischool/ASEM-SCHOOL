Our Vision & Legacy

Akshara Siddhi E.M. School was established with a noble vision by our founder, Mr. Sanapala Swamibabu Garu. It was his dream to uplift rural areas by providing high-quality educational facilities.

This legacy is carried forward by Mr. Sanapala Satyanarayana, specializing in IIT foundation coaching.
